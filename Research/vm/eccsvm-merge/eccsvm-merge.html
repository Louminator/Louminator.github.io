High Order Vortex Methods with Deforming Elliptical Gaussian Blobs 2. Merging.

Louis F. Rossi

Abstract:

This manuscript derives a merging algorithm for elliptical Gaussian basis functions such as are used in high order vortex methods using deformable computational elements. This merging algorithm relies upon several estimates for pairwise differences between basis functions. The resulting merging algorithm is local, fast and efficient, and provides a practical means of removing extraneous degrees of freedom from vortex calculations. In the case of traditional vortex methods with rigid computational elements, the algorithm discussed here offers some improvement over the existing merging algorithm for radially symmetric vortex elements.
