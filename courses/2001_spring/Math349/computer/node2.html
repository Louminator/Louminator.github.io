Preparing your classroom account

You should configure Netscape to go directly to this course's web site. After starting Netscape, go to the URL

http://www.math.udel.edu/rossi/courses/2001_spring/Math349/

Next, click on Edit > Preferences. Choose Navigator from the menu and click on Use Current Page and then OK. Now, whenever you run netscape in the classroom, it will start on the course home page. You might also choose to bookmark the course home page when on other computer systems that you use.
